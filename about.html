Our Mission and Vision

Mission Our Purpose

The mission is to build connection and collaborative opportunities on a global
platform by instilling a deep sense of ambition. This will
balance the playing field on an international level
in education and professional development. ILM Educational
Consultants seeks to support the application of a growth mindset and by
increasing knowledge, we hope to augment equality and opportunities
universally. We endeavor to support, nourish and develop
leaders to excel globally.

Vision

Where we want to be

ILM Educational Consultants aims to equip and prepare individuals
and organisations to lead the way in business, entrepreneurial
and social engagement around the globe; empowering
others with inspirational, proactive and effective
leadership.

Company History
Tata Consultancy Services Limited (TCS):

is an Indian multinational information technology (IT) services and consulting company headquartered in Mumbai.[7][8] It is a part of the Tata Group and operates in 150 locations across 46 countries.[9] In March 2024, TCS reported that it had 601,546 employees worldwide.[10][11] TCS is the second-largest Indian company by market capitalization, the most valuable IT service brands worldwide, and the top Big Tech (India) company.[12][13][14] In 2012, it was the world's second-largest user of U.S. H-1B visas.[15]

As of 2021, it was ranked seventh on the Fortune India 500 list.[16] In September 2021, TCS recorded a market capitalization of US$200 billion, making it the first Indian IT tech company to do so.[17][18][19][20][21] In 2016–2017, parent company Tata Sons owned 72.05% of TCS[22] and more than 70% of Tata Sons' dividends were generated by TCS.[23]
